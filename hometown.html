New York City, New York

Location

New York City is located on the US east coast. It's an island southeast of New York and east of New Jersey. NYC is about 1 hour and 45 minutes away from Philadelphia, PA.

About

Parts of the City

NYC has 5 sections, known as boroughs. These are Brooklyn, the Bronx, Queens, Staten Island, and Manhattan. Each borough is home to its own county. Manhattan is the most well known of these 5, and is what people typically associate with NYC.

Sporting Events

The top sports in NYC are football (New York Giants and Jets), basketball (New York Knicks and Brooklyn Nets), and baseball (New York Yankees and Mets).

Population

In 2020, the population of NYC was over 8 million people.

Things To Do

  • Visit the Empire State Building
  • Visit Central Park
  • Visit Times Square
  • Go to the Museum of Natural History
  • Go to the Metropolitan Museum of Art
  • Go to Broadway
  • Visit the World Trade Center Memorial
  • See a sports game
  • Go to the Bronx Zoo
  • Go to Wall Street
  • Take a ferry to visit the Statue of Liberty and Ellis Island
